Sacraments & Pastoral Care

Confession (Reconciliation)

Confession is offered at the following times:

  • Sundays, 2:30 PM — 30 minutes before Mass in Collinsville
  • During office hours — Monday through Thursday at the Martinsville office

For pastoral emergencies, please call (276) 388-9004.

Anointing of the Sick

The Anointing of the Sick is available during office hours at the Martinsville office, and by appointment for those who are homebound or hospitalized.

Please call (276) 388-9004 to arrange a visit or appointment.
