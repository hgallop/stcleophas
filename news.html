Welcome to our new parish website

We are pleased to launch the new Saint Cleophas Outreach Ministry website. Here you will find our Mass and prayer schedule, information about our ministries, and ways to get in touch. Please share the site with friends and family who may be interested in joining us for worship.

Each Wednesday

Wednesday Worship Day

Join us every Wednesday at First Baptist Church in Martinsville for Morning Prayer at 9:30 AM (live streamed), Holy Eucharist at noon, Bible Study at 1:00 PM, and Evening Prayer at 4:30 PM (live streamed). All are welcome at each gathering.

Ongoing

Pastoral Care & Sacraments Available During Office Hours

Confession and the Anointing of the Sick are available during office hours at our Martinsville office (23 Starling Ave). Office hours are Monday 9–5, Tuesday 12–5, and Wednesday–Thursday 9–5. For urgent pastoral needs, please call (276) 388-9004.
